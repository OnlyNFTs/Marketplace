Our Mission

OnlyNFTs strives to create a platform for Content Creators with cryptocurrency and utilising NFT's. Our platform will make it easy for Content Creators to promote and engage with fans, turn their content into NFT's and sell them with royalties for life. Fans will be rewarded for early adoption and use of our platform and for holding $ONFTs tokens.

Tokenomics

$ONFTs has a 7% transaction Fee as follows.

2% Holders

2% of ALL transactions will be redistributed porporotionaly to holders.

2% Liquidity

2% of ALL transactions will automatically be paired and added to liquidity.

2% Platform

2% of ALL transactions will be converted to BNB and sent to platform wallet for marketing/platform updates ETC.

1% Burn

1% of ALL transactions will be burnt forever reducing the supply.
